Vision

  
  
    

Starting with their very own identity, we want children to know that they are born to shine for Jesus – to be a burning and shining lamp like John the Baptist in the darkest hours of the endtimes—and to prepare for Jesus' second coming. We expect them to possess the quintessence of Daniel's life and to live like him: loyal, bold and uncompromising, focused on seeking to understand the things in the endtimes, and to walk in His revelations, love Him and love one another till the very end.
